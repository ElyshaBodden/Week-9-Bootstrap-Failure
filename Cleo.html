Meet Cleopatra a.k.a Cleo

Cleo is 4 years old and weighs about 8.5 lbs.

She is a June baby. This makes her a gemini.

Though she is physically a dog, I like to think that she was a cat in a past life.

  • Only plays with cat toys
  • Likes her food cut in miniscule pieces
  • Lounges on the backs of couches
  • Drinks out of fountains, faucets, or bath tubs

Cleo was not my first dog but she is my first psuedo child.
